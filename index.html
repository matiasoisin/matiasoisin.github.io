Who I am

Lapland,
from above

Based in Rovaniemi, I offer drone cinematography across Finnish Lapland — a region that most photographers can only dream of working in. Frozen rivers, aurora skies, reindeer herds moving through birch forests.

I work with tourism companies, safari operators, event organisers, real estate agents, and individual athletes looking to build sponsor packages. Fast turnaround, professional delivery, and footage that actually looks like Lapland.
